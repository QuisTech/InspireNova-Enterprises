The SME Security Problem (Nigeria Focus)

  • Nigerian SMEs face growing digital threats without proper security infrastructure.
  • Business owners lack awareness and tools to assess their security vulnerabilities.
  • Traditional security solutions are too expensive and complex for small businesses.
  • Security breaches cost Nigerian businesses billions of Naira annually.

✅ There is a critical need for affordable, accessible security tools designed specifically for Nigerian SMEs.

🛡️ NaijaBiz Shield

Digital Resilience Platform for Nigerian SMEs

What It Does

  1. SMEs complete a 10-minute security assessment.
  2. AI analyzes business vulnerabilities across multiple categories.
  3. Generates personalized risk score & recommendations.
  4. Provides current threat alerts specific to Nigerian businesses.
  5. Delivers comprehensive PDF security reports with actionable steps.
