🧠 TalentMatch AI

AI Talent Screening & Interview Automation Tool

What It Does

  1. Recruiter enters a job description.
  2. AI extracts ideal skills & candidate profile.
  3. Candidate CVs or LinkedIn profiles are uploaded.
  4. AI (via OpenAI + Pinecone) scores & ranks candidates.
  5. Custom interview questions are auto-generated based on candidate gaps.
